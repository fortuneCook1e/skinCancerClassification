Computer-aided Diagnosis (CAD) System for Diagnosing Skin Cancer

Instructions

  1. Click on "Choose File" to upload an image.
  2. View the uploaded image in the left preview box.
  3. Click "Predict" to process the image.
  4. See the result in the right preview box.
    • The blue boundary indicates the region of the lesion
    • The predicted class
    • The confidence score
  5. If needed, click "Clear" to reset and start over.
